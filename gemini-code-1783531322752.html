Who We Are

A Legacy of Trust & Excellence in Transportation

Royal Havard Marketing Solution Private Limited is a premier transportation and logistics company headquartered in Bihar, India. We specialize in the safe and efficient transportation of petroleum products, fertilizers, industrial raw materials, and consumer goods for some of the nation's largest corporations.

With a modern fleet of tankers, cargo vehicles, and a team of highly trained professionals, we ensure every consignment reaches its destination on time and in perfect condition. Our commitment to safety, compliance, and customer satisfaction has made us the preferred logistics partner for industry giants like IOCL, HURL, NTPC, and PepsiCo.

What We Do

Comprehensive Transport Solutions

From petroleum to packaged goods — we move what powers the nation with precision and reliability.

Petroleum Transport

Safe and compliant transportation of crude oil, refined petroleum products, and LPG for IOCL and HPCL across multiple states with GPS-tracked tanker fleets.

Industrial Raw Materials

Bulk transportation of coal, fly ash, limestone, and other industrial raw materials for power plants like NTPC, ensuring uninterrupted supply chains.

Fertilizer Logistics

End-to-end logistics for fertilizer distribution on behalf of HURL (Hindustan Urvarak & Rasayan Limited), covering primary and last-mile delivery.

FMCG Distribution

Temperature-conscious transport and warehousing distribution support for PepsiCo beverages, ensuring freshness and timely shelf replenishment.

Contract Logistics

Dedicated fleet solutions and long-term contract logistics for enterprises seeking a reliable, single-source transportation partner.

GPS Fleet Tracking

Real-time vehicle tracking, route optimization, and digital proof-of-delivery for complete shipment visibility and transparency.
